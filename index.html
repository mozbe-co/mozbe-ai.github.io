Common questions

Does this give legal advice?

No. Mozbe AI never provides legal advice. It gathers information, schedules consultations, and routes messages to your team.

It’s designed to support your team by handling after-hours and overflow, so your staff can focus on high-value work.

What about confidentiality and HIPAA?

We follow confidentiality-first workflows and can sign BAAs on request for health-related PHI captured during intake.

Which calendars and CRMs do you support?

Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, and common legal CRMs via email or API integration.
